Our Manufacturing Process

From concept to delivery, we streamline the journey.

1. Consultation & Design

Share your ideas with us. Our experts refine your designs to match your brand's style and goals.

2. Quote & Approval

We provide a detailed quote. Once approved, we prepare for the next steps.

3. Sample Creation

A physical sample is produced for your review via video call or shipment to ensure perfect fit.

4. Bulk Production

After sample approval, we begin full-scale manufacturing maintaining high quality standards.

5. Shipping & Delivery

Your custom apparel is packed and shipped, arriving at your doorstep in 5-7 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of sports clothing do you manufacture?

We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of clothing, including jerseys, tracksuits, t-shirts, leggings, shorts, and performance wear for various sports like soccer, basketball, and fitness.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our minimum order quantity is 15 pieces. We offer competitive pricing for bulk orders, ideal for teams and retailers.

What is the turnaround time?

Typical turnaround time is 12-15 days from design approval. Rush orders can be accommodated for an additional fee.

Can I customize with my own logos?

Yes! Full customization is available including logos, names, and numbers using high-quality printing or embroidery.
