About Us

Canzon Sport is a leading custom clothing manufacturer, specializing in high-quality sportswear and athletic apparel. With years of experience and a passion for excellence, we deliver top-notch products tailored to our clients' specific needs.

Company Overview

Founded in 2010, Canzon Sport has grown to become a trusted name in custom clothing manufacturing. Our state-of-the-art facilities and skilled workforce allow us to produce a wide range of sportswear, from team uniforms to performance gear. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order quantity is 15 pieces per design. This allows us to maintain efficiency in production while offering competitive pricing.
What is the turnaround time for orders?
Typical turnaround time for orders is 12-15 Days from design approval to shipping. Rush orders may be accommodated for an additional fee, subject to our current production schedule.
Do you offer design services?
Yes, we have an in-house design team that can help bring your ideas to life. We offer design services at competitive rates, ensuring your custom clothing meets your exact specifications.
